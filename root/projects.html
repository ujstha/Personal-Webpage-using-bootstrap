I Got Mojo Oy

I Got Mojo Oy

This was a group project. There was four members in my group. The idea of the project was to come up with a business idea and execute it to mobile app or website or anything we like. Our group chose to go with the website. The business idea was to develop a web application version of show like America's Got Talent. Basically, a website where talented people could upload their audio or video in our site and we have the partnership with big production companies.

The website was full stack and built using ReactJS as a front-end, Firebase as back-end, Material-UI as designing and Visual Studio as a text editor, nodejs as an material installer and github as a version control system. There was no restriction for the material to use in this project.
